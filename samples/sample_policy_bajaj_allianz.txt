SECTION 2: COVERAGE BENEFITS / कवरेज लाभ
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 IN-PATIENT HOSPITALIZATION (IPD)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Coverage for hospitalization expenses when admitted for more than 24 hours:
• Room, Boarding & Nursing charges
• Surgeon, Anesthetist, Medical Practitioner fees
• Medicines & Drugs
• Diagnostic tests
• ICU/ICCU charges
• Operation Theatre charges
• Cost of implants, stents, pacemakers

2.2 PRE-HOSPITALIZATION EXPENSES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Covered for 60 days prior to hospitalization
• Includes consultations, diagnostics, medicines related to admission

2.3 POST-HOSPITALIZATION EXPENSES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Covered for 90 days after discharge
• Follow-up consultations, medicines, diagnostics

2.4 DAY CARE PROCEDURES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• 541+ Day Care procedures covered
• No 24-hour hospitalization required
• Includes: Dialysis, Chemotherapy, Cataract, Angioplasty, etc.

2.5 DOMICILIARY HOSPITALIZATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Treatment at home when hospital admission not possible
• Minimum 3 days treatment required
• Subject to medical necessity

2.6 AMBULANCE CHARGES
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Up to ₹5,000/- per hospitalization
• Includes road ambulance and air ambulance (as per limits)

2.7 AYUSH TREATMENT
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Siddha, Homeopathy covered
• In recognized AYUSH hospitals
• Same limits as allopathic treatment

SECTION 4: WAITING PERIODS / प्रतीक्षा अवधि
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 INITIAL WAITING PERIOD: 30 DAYS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• No claim payable for any illness during first 30 days
• Exception: Accidental injuries covered from Day 1

4.2 PRE-EXISTING DISEASE WAITING PERIOD: 48 MONTHS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Diseases existing before policy inception
• 48 months waiting from first policy date
• Covered after waiting period completion
• Continuous renewal required

4.3 SPECIFIC DISEASE WAITING PERIOD: 24 MONTHS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Following diseases have 24-month waiting period:
• Cataract
• Joint Replacement (Hip, Knee)
• Hernia
• Hydrocele
• Benign Prostatic Hypertrophy
• Hysterectomy (for menorrhagia/fibroid)
• Fissure/Fistula/Piles
• Sinusitis
• ENT disorders
• Kidney/Urinary stones
• Gallbladder stones
• Uterine Fibroid

4.4 MATERNITY WAITING PERIOD: 36 MONTHS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Maternity expenses covered after 36 months
• Newborn baby covered for 90 days from birth

SECTION 5: EXCLUSIONS / अपवर्जन
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 PERMANENT EXCLUSIONS (Never Covered)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Cosmetic or plastic surgery (unless medically necessary)
2. Dental treatment (unless due to accident)
3. Spectacles, contact lenses, hearing aids
4. Infertility treatment (IVF, IUI, etc.)
5. Intentional self-injury or attempted suicide
6. Treatment arising from alcohol/drug abuse
7. War, nuclear contamination
8. Adventure sports injuries
9. Vaccination (except post-bite treatment)
10. Weight management/obesity surgery
11. Congenital diseases (external)
12. Sexual dysfunction treatment
13. Change of sex/gender treatment
14. Sleep apnea treatment (unless associated with illness)
15. Experimental or unproven treatments

5.2 ITEMS NOT PAYABLE (Non-Medical Items)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
As per IRDAI standard list:
• Toiletries, cosmetics
• Guest meals, telephone charges
• Admission kit, gown
• Slippers, tissue paper
• TV, laundry charges
• Registration, documentation charges
• Service charges, surcharges

SECTION 7: CLAIM PROCEDURE / दावा प्रक्रिया
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

7.1 CASHLESS CLAIMS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Visit network hospital
2. Show health card at admission desk
3. Hospital sends pre-auth request to TPA
4. TPA approves/queries within 1-2 hours
5. Treatment provided; bill settled directly

7.2 REIMBURSEMENT CLAIMS
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Intimate within 24 hours of admission
2. Pay hospital bills upfront
3. Submit claim within 15 days of discharge
4. Documents required:
   □ Claim form (duly filled)
   □ Discharge summary
   □ Final hospital bill (itemized)
   □ All investigation reports
   □ Pharmacy bills with prescription
   □ Doctor's notes/consultation records
   □ Payment receipts
   □ Photo ID proof

7.3 CLAIM SETTLEMENT TIMELINE (IRDAI Mandated)
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
• Acknowledgment: 3 working days
• Query (if any): Within 15 days
• Settlement: Within 30 days of complete documents
• Interest: 2% above bank rate if delayed
