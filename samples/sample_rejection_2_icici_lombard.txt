REASON FOR REJECTION:
Pre-Authorization/Intimation not obtained for planned hospitalization

Dear Policyholder,

We regret to inform you that your claim has been rejected for the following reason:

As per your policy terms and conditions (Section 5.4 - Claim Procedure), 
pre-authorization/prior intimation is mandatory for all planned hospitalizations. 
Our records indicate that no pre-authorization was obtained before the admission 
on 05 January, 2026.

Policy Terms Quoted:
"For planned hospitalization, the insured must obtain pre-authorization from the 
TPA at least 48 hours before admission. Failure to obtain pre-authorization may 
result in denial of cashless facility and/or claim rejection."

Investigation Findings:
• No pre-authorization request found in our system
• Hospital did not intimate TPA before admission
• Claim submitted as reimbursement after discharge

Note: Mental health treatment is covered under your policy as per Mental Healthcare 
Act, 2017 and IRDAI guidelines on parity. However, procedural requirements like 
pre-authorization still apply.
