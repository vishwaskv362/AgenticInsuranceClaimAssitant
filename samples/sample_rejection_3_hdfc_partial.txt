DETAILED EXPLANATION
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Dear Mr. Venkatesh Rao,

Your claim for CABG surgery has been processed. However, due to the following 
reasons, a partial payment of ₹5,89,000/- is being made instead of the full 
claim of ₹8,50,000/-:

1. ROOM RENT SUB-LIMIT BREACH (₹56,000/- deducted)
   Your policy has a room rent sub-limit of ₹8,000/- per day for a ₹15 lakh 
   Sum Insured. You occupied a room costing ₹15,000/- per day.
   
   Excess per day: ₹15,000 - ₹8,000 = ₹7,000/-
   For 8 days: ₹7,000 x 8 = ₹56,000/-

2. PROPORTIONATE DEDUCTION (₹1,87,600/- deducted)
   As per policy clause 4.5(b), when room rent exceeds the entitled limit, 
   all other charges are reduced proportionately.
   
   Eligible Room Rate / Actual Room Rate = ₹8,000 / ₹15,000 = 53.33%
   Deduction Percentage = 100% - 53.33% = 46.67%
   (Applied on surgery, ICU, medicines, etc.)
   
   Note: We have applied 22% as per claim assessment.

3. NON-PAYABLE ITEMS (₹17,400/- deducted)
   As per IRDAI standard list of non-payable items and policy terms, the 
   following are not covered:
   - Administrative charges
   - Consumables (gloves, syringes)
   - Guest meals and amenities
   - Convenience items
